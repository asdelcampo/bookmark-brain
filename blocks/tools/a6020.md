Field Theory is a powerful command-line interface designed to solve the problem of scattered digital knowledge by syncing and storing all your X/Twitter bookmarks locally. It allows users to perform deep searches, classify content using LLMs, and build an interconnected, wiki-style knowledge base from their saved tweets. Its key differentiator is its ability to integrate this private, structured knowledge directly into AI agents (like Claude Code or Codex) via shell access, turning passive bookmarks into active, queryable assets.

This is a comprehensive, open-source AI suite designed as a powerful alternative to commercial platforms like Higgsfield. It offers over 200 models for various tasks, including text-to-image, image-to-video, and professional lip-syncing. Its key differentiator is that it is entirely self-hosted and free, allowing users to bypass subscription costs and maintain full control over their generated content.

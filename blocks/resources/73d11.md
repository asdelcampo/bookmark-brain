Awesome Design MD is a comprehensive collection of reverse-engineered design systems taken from industry-leading companies such as Apple, Spotify, and Airbnb. It solves the problem of starting UI development from a blank slate by providing pre-packaged, high-fidelity design components. Developers can integrate these systems into their projects, allowing AI agents or tools to build UIs that instantly adopt the aesthetic and structure of established, billion-dollar applications.

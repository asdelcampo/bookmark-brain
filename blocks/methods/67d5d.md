This advisory explains that technical errors encountered on X.com are often linked to conflicts with privacy-related browser extensions. The primary solution is to temporarily disable these extensions and try reloading the page. This method is a critical first step in diagnosing platform-specific issues that are not related to the core functionality of the website.

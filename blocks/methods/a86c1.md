This strategy advocates for basing all sales and marketing efforts on actual market feedback rather than guesswork. By analyzing what customers are genuinely saying, businesses can pinpoint the most effective messaging, content angles, and objection handling points. This data-first approach ensures that your sales process directly addresses the real pain points and language used by your target market.

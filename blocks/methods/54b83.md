This content showcases the capability of Figma to move beyond simple static mockups into complex, interactive prototypes. By utilizing features such as hands tracking, conditionals, and linked assets, designers can simulate sophisticated user experiences. It is valuable because it allows non-developers to prototype highly advanced interactions, significantly accelerating the early design and testing phase of a product.
